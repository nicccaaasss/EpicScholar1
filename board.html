How to Play Chess

  • Win: Checkmate the opponent's King.
  • Move: Click a piece to select, then click a destination.
  • Pawn: Forward 1 (or 2 first move), capture diagonal.
  • Knight: L-shape jump.
  • Bishop: Diagonals.
  • Rook: Straight lines.
  • Queen: Any direction.
  • King: 1 square any direction.
